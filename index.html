About NATALE 2025

NATALE 2025, organized by St. Sebastian's Central College, Katuneriya, will be held from 19th to 23rd December 2025 at the college grounds.

This five-day celebration will feature an educational and commercial exhibition, food stalls, games, art displays, and daily grand musical shows with leading Sri Lankan bands including Wayo, Yaka Crew, Sanidapa, Flashback, and Avetar.

With an expected audience of over 125,000 students, families, and community members, Natale 2025 is set to be a vibrant celebration of Christmas values, culture, creativity, and community spirit.
